# Making a Text Editor

I have been planning on craeting a user based project for quite a while. Altough it is not my greates goal in life to make apps for people, but the number of utilities and unknowns surrounding text editors always felt mysterious. I had used `nano` for a while, some of my professors used `vi` (not `vim` or `nvim`, just `vi`) and I had found `nvim` attractive from all the ramble about it on reddit. So I did some research and found [snaptoken's text editor tutorial](https://viewsourcecode.org/snaptoken/), and I decided to give it a go. And though I did start to remake `kilo` following his steps my interest faded over a couple of days (I was going slow, ok?), and decided to drop it. 

Recently, I gained back the interest to continue (this is something that happens to me when a lot of work accumulates, I take on more projects). But I was planning on doing it on Rust, but that idea didn't work as well. I had just picked up C, and now kilo was not gibberish anymore. So, I decided to use all tools available to start a project like that on my own. I will try documenting the process, as long as it may take.
